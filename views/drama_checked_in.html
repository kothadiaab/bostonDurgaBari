DRAMA: CHECKED-IN

We tease, we laugh, we follow, we vent out, we embrace, we reject but we can not ignore it. Social media has created an evolution in integrated social system and established a spider net of human network. “Checked –In “, an original play written and directed by Jayati Banerjee takes a non-critique and comical view towards human psychology and its adaptive capability under strong influence of social media.
